Vela

Vela is a personalised weather forecast application, specifically designed to help water-sport aficionados stay safe & plan a relaxing day on the water
…even if they lack the experience!

  • The Client - Student project at careerfoundry

  • Tools - Pen & Paper, Google Forms, Optimal Sort, Usability Hub, Skype, Powerpoint, Balsamiq, Adobe XD

  • My Role - The entire product design from research to conception, visualisation & testing

  • Project Length - 7 months

Style Guide

Our brand aims to invoke calmness, curiosity &confidence in it’s message. With the use of soothing shades, an adventurous voice and essential information we provide our users - from beginners to pros - with a space to enjoy their water sports safely.

User Flow

My user flows formed a base for the initial wireframes. These helped to visualise my ideas before creating the low-fidelity prototypes.

Wireframing & Prototyping

After much trial & error, crazy 8s & iteration, the low-fidelity prototypes were ready. I followed up on these in the digital space by using online tools such as Balsamiq for the medium-fidelity prototypes. I then utilised Adobe XD for the medium to high-fidelity prototypes & eventually the test prototype.

Design Decisions

With the foundation of my design established, it was time to begin the refinement process. In order to get VELA “market ready” the aesthetic needed to be smoothed & sharpened. With the help of preference tests & peer reviews, I meticulously stepped through every possible user path, tweaking & tailoring as I went.
